\begin{ex}
Let $T(\begin{bmatrix}
x\\y
\end{bmatrix})=\begin{bmatrix}
x+3y\\
-x+y\\
2x+y
\end{bmatrix}$.
\[im(T)=\{t\begin{bmatrix}
1\\-1\\2
\end{bmatrix}+s\begin{bmatrix}
3\\
1\\
1
\end{bmatrix}\ |\ t,s\in \R\}\]
We know that this is contained in the plane $3x-5y-4z$.\\
To see that $P\subseteq im(T)$, we solve
\[t+3s=x;\ -t+6=s;\ \]
\end{ex}
